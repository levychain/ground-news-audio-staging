Anti-Pitfall Rules

These prevent the first pass from sounding overproduced or templated.
No Wallpaper

Music cannot sit under everything. Dry narration is a feature when Daniel needs authority.

Emotion Match

Human stories get warm beds. Analysis gets clean beds. Tension appears only when stakes rise.

Voice First

Every bed must duck below speech and avoid rhythmic or melodic clutter under key lines.

Vary the Shape

Use stingers, beds, swells, dry gaps, and reflection lifts so cues do not feel copied.

One Primary Bed

Only one main music bed can run at a time, except for explicit timed crossfades.
